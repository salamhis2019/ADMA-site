What is hate Speech?

Hate speech is defined as public speech that expresses hate or encourages violence towards a person or group based on something such as race, religion, sex, or sexual orientation.

There is a difference between hate spech and a rude comment. While a post can be rude, it does not mean it is hate speech, thus its classification requires careful consideration.


For a rude comment to be considered hate speech, it should express hatred or hostility toward a particular group or class of persons.
